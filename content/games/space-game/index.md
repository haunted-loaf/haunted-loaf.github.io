# Space Game

A game in the style of Frontier: Elite II and, to a lesser extent, Elite: Dangerous.

* Newtonian physics

* Procedurally generated universe

* Modular ship construction

* Interesting factions

* Main gameplay loops:

  * Resource extraction
  * Trading
  * Exploration
  * Combat
  * Base building
  * Business management
  * Empire management

* Different "career" paths:

  * Merchant
  * Military officer
  * Pioneer
  * Pirate
  * Politician
