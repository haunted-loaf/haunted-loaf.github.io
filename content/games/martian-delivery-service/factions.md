# Factions

Many of these factions overlap. There may be politist Earthling homesteaders, or Martian corporatists.

## Backgrounds

### Earthlings

People who consider themselves citizens of Earth. Some are on Mars for work, some for pleasure, some out of necessitity.

### Martians

People who consider themselves citizens of Mars. Many Martians were born on Mars, or have lived there for decades.

### Resettled

People transported to Mars as part of the UN's excess labour resettlement program (UN-ELRP), a program designed to help address hyperunderemployment on Earth by sending people to Mars.

## Economic Factions

### Communists

Communists are supporters of a Martian society based on common ownership of the means of production. Many communists regard Mars as an opportunity to build a new society unburdened by Earth's cultural baggage.

### Corporatists

Corporatists are supporters of corporate control of Mars. Typically Earthlings, they regard Mars as principally a venue for economic exploitation, not as a place for humanity to live.

Due to their differences, corporatists are often at odds with Martians. But wealthy Martians may also be corporatists.

## Political Factions

### Colonists

Colonists are supporters of the idea that Mars should be ruled from Earth. Colonists don't think Mars should have an independent, sovereign government.

### Homesteaders

Homesteaders are people who value independence and self-reliance, and have chosen to live in relative isolation from the major populations of the bases and settlements.

A homestead generally consists of a family or group living in a habitat with a greenhouse and a solar array. They aim to be self-sufficient in their basic needs. Most homesteaders are not foolish, however, and connect with others to acquire things they can't make themselves, like medicines or electronics.

Homesteaders are usually Martians. Many first-generation Martians came to Mars pursuing a dream of life free from government oversight.

### Politists

Politists are supporters of the Martian Polity, a proto-typical government developing within Martian society. They believe in independence from Earth under a Martian government, although there is still much debate over the form it should take.

The status of the Polity varies by settlement. Some settlements are fully Polity-run, while in others the Polity is a suppressed underground movement.
