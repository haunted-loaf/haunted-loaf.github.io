Factions

Martian Confederation

A loose alliance of, primarily, homesteaders, the Martian Confederation is a political movement that seeks to establish a decentralised, self-governing Martian state.

Martian Home Rule

A movement for Martian independence, the Martian Home Rule movement seeks to establish a Martian state. Most MHR advocates call for a strong centralised state, but there is still much disagreement on the form it should take.

Colonial Realism

A movement to maintain the status quo. Colonial Realism seeks to keep Mars under the control of the Union of Earth.

Movement for Planetary Union

A movement for a united Mars to join the Union of Earth as a member state. This is a sort of hybrid of the Martian Home Rule and Colonial movements. It has limited support, mostly from Earthers.
