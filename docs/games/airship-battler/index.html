Airship Battler

A game about airships fighting.

Set in a steampunk alternative history where fossil fuels don't exist. Without oil the internal combustion engine was not developed until much later, and remains a curiosity.

Airships are steam powered and burn wood. They are the most effective way to move across unknown, hostile, or dry terrain.

Steamships remain effective on the ocean and large rivers, but are not the subject of the game. (Probably.)
